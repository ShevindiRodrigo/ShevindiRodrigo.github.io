About Me

I’m Shevindi, a passionate Business/Data Analyst with a strong interest in transforming data into meaningful insights that help drive decision-making and solve real-world problems. My journey has been shaped by my pursuit of a deeper understanding of how data can be leveraged to improve processes, optimize performance, and bring value to businesses.
With a background in Computer Science and a Master’s in Information Technology specializing in Business Analytics, I have honed my skills in data analysis, visualization, and data-driven strategies. I thrive in environments where I can dive deep into datasets, uncover hidden trends, and translate complex data into actionable insights.
What excites me most is the intersection of technology and business strategy—using data analytics to uncover patterns, predict trends, and inform critical decisions. Whether it's improving operational efficiency, supporting business growth, or exploring new ways to apply analytics, I’m always eager to learn and grow within the dynamic world of data. I am continuously exploring new techniques and tools to enhance my ability to analyze and interpret data, and I am passionate about using my skills to make a tangible impact. For me, data isn’t just numbers—it’s a powerful tool that can unlock new possibilities and drive change.
